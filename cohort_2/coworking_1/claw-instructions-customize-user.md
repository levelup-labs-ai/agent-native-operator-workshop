# Customize USER.md

Walk the user through customizing their USER.md identity file. Assume SOUL.md and AGENTS.md are already in place in the workspace. Do not modify them.

If USER.md already has content, ask the user whether to start fresh or refine what's there.

## Step 1: confirm identity files are loaded

Read SOUL.md and AGENTS.md from the workspace. Confirm both exist and are non-empty. Tell the user one short line per file: who you are as a persona (from SOUL.md) and what operating procedures are in place (from AGENTS.md). Do not dump the full files.

If either file is missing, stop and tell the user to install them before continuing.

## Step 2: walk USER.md sections in 2 batches of 2

Ask the four sections in two batches. Wait for both answers in each batch before moving to the next.

**Batch 1 — Who you are:**

> Two quick questions:
> 1. **Who** — name, pronouns, timezone, location.
> 2. **Contact** — when do you check messages, your latency tolerance for replies.

Once you have both answers, write the Who and Contact sections of USER.md. Tell the user in one line what you captured (e.g., "Saved: Aishwarya, she/her, Pacific, based in SF; checks messages midday and evening, 2-hour latency tolerance.").

**Batch 2 — What you do and how you talk:**

> Two more:
> 3. **Role** — one or two sentences about what you do.
> 4. **Style** — length, format, tone preferences; what to do when you don't know something.

Once you have both answers, write the Role and Style sections. Tell the user in one line what you captured.

## Step 3: read back the result

After all four sections are written, give the user a 4-bullet summary of what you now know about them. Ask if anything's wrong. If they correct something, update USER.md and tell them in plain English what you changed.

## Step 4: stop

Stop when the user confirms the summary is accurate.
