# Q3 Pricing memo

Date: Apr 13, 2026
Author: Maya
Audience: Foxglove team (Diego, Priya, Tomás)

## Decision

Bumping list price 20% on new logos starting Jul 1, 2026.

- Starter: $1,000 to $1,200 per seat per month
- Growth: $1,400 to $1,680 per seat per month
- Enterprise: priced bespoke (no change in publishing)

Existing customers are grandfathered through their next renewal. Q3 pricing applies on renewal.

## Rationale

Win rate at current pricing is too high (above 70% from qualified inbound). That's the textbook signal of under-pricing. Three signals stacking:

1. Win rate above the healthy 30 to 50% band.
2. Two of the last three closed deals didn't negotiate on price at all.
3. Two prospects last month tried to upgrade tiers within 60 days, suggesting we left budget on the table.

Pricing conversations with Erin Vasquez at Dovetail and Rajiv Mehta at Acme this past month both indicated 20% headroom before fit drops off.

## What this means for in-flight deals

- **Acme expansion** (currently being scoped): still at Growth $1,400. Expansion closes before the cutover, so locked.
- **Dovetail** (new logo, in discovery): will be priced at the new Growth $1,680 once they ask for a proposal.
- **Cresta** (prospect): no exception. Their budget question lands at the new number.
